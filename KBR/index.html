Frosted Records

Frosted Records is home to an all-star roster of multi-platinum, award-winning legends and developing superstar artists.

Labeled as one of the fastest rising record labels by Rolling Stone, Pitchfork, and Loudwire, Frosted Records is home to a diverse number of musical artists. With two recent signees being rising Michigan rock bands, Sidewater and V*A*S*E, the Midwestern label has the attention of the national press, with more artists to follow. The hot spot of Michigan rock is not to be outdone by the electric multi-instrumentalistm of rising pop artist, King Bayley. Full of a diverse range of artists, Frosted Records is sure to be a hit for all your music loving friends. Take a look around, view our catalouge, and listen to some of the leading creatives in the Midwest, and beyond.
